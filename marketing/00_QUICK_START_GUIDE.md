## ðŸ’¡ Pro Tips for Maximum Impact

### **1. The Hook is Everything**
First 3 seconds determine if people watch. Test these hooks:
- "Stop paying $29/month for invoice software..."
- "What if managing your business was this easy..."
- "Tired of spreadsheet chaos?"

### **2. Show Real Results**
Use actual numbers from your app in ads:
- "Join 10,000+ businesses"
- "Save 5 hours per week"
- "Rated 4.8/5 stars"

### **3. Feature Your USP**
Your biggest advantage: **No Monthly Fees**
- Make this prominent in EVERY ad
- Compare to competitor pricing
- Calculate savings: "$29/mo Ã— 12 = $348 wasted"

### **4. Use Captions Always**
85% watch without sound:
- Add text overlays to videos
- Summarize key points
- Make ads understandable on mute

### **5. Test Everything**
Create variations:
- 3 different headlines
- 3 different images/videos
- 3 different audiences
- Run all 9 combinations, find winner

### **6. Retarget Heavily**
90% don't convert first time:
- Install Facebook Pixel
- Set up App Events in Firebase
- Retarget people who viewed Play Store page
- Show them testimonials, reviews, features

### **7. Seasonal Opportunities**
- **New Year:** "Start 2025 organized"
- **Tax Season:** "Get your invoices in order"
- **Black Friday:** Special launch discount
- **Small Business Saturday:** Target SMB owners

### **8. Leverage User Content**
Once you have users:
- Ask for video testimonials
- Offer $20 credit for review
- Share success stories in ads
- Create case studies

## ðŸš¨ Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **No Clear CTA** - Every ad needs "Download Now" or similar
2. **Too Much Text** - Keep copy under 125 characters for Facebook
3. **Poor Demo Data** - Never show "$0" or empty screens
4. **No Testing** - Don't run just 1 ad, test variations
5. **Ignoring Metrics** - Check daily, optimize weekly
6. **Targeting Too Broad** - Start specific, expand later
7. **No Budget** - Need at least $10/day per platform
8. **Giving Up Too Soon** - Takes 2-4 weeks to see patterns
9. **No Mobile Optimization** - Most views are mobile
10. **Forgetting App Store Optimization** - Ads drive traffic, ASO converts
