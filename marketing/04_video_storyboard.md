2. **Click-Through Rate (CTR)**
   - Goal: >2% for social ads
   - Measures: How strong is the CTA?

   - Goal: >5% of clicks â†’ installs
   - Measures: Overall ad effectiveness

5. **Watch Time %**
   - Goal: >60% average watch
   - Measures: Is video engaging throughout?

## Final Tips for Success

1. **Hook in 3 Seconds:** First frame must grab attention
2. **Show, Don't Tell:** Demonstrate the app, not just talk about it
3. **Clear Value Prop:** "No monthly fees" is your killer feature
4. **Social Proof:** Include ratings/reviews for credibility
5. **Strong CTA:** Make next step obvious (Download Now)
6. **Test Variations:** Try different hooks, different CTAs
7. **Mobile-First:** 90% will watch on phone, design for small screen
8. **Captions Always:** Most watch without sound
9. **Brand Consistency:** Use your exact app colors and fonts
10. **Iterate:** First version won't be perfect, keep improving
