## Voiceover Recording Guide

### Voice Characteristics
- **Tone:** Friendly but professional
- **Pace:** Moderate (not too fast or slow)
- **Energy:** Enthusiastic but not over-the-top
- **Style:** Conversational, like talking to a friend

### Recording Setup
- **Microphone:** USB condenser mic (Blue Yeti, Audio-Technica AT2020)
- **Environment:** Quiet room, add soft materials to reduce echo
- **Software:** Audacity (free), Adobe Audition, or GarageBand
- **Settings:** 44.1kHz, 24-bit WAV

### AI Voiceover Alternatives
If hiring voice talent isn't in budget:

1. **ElevenLabs** (Best quality, realistic)
   - Voice: "Adam" for authoritative, "Josh" for friendly
   - Cost: ~$5-30/month

2. **Descript Overdub** (Good for quick iterations)
   - Clone your own voice
   - Cost: ~$12/month

3. **Murf.ai** (Easy to use)
   - Voice: "Ken" or "Natalie"
   - Cost: ~$19/month

4. **Google Cloud Text-to-Speech** (Free tier available)
   - Voice: "en-US-Neural2-D" or "en-US-Neural2-F"

## Video Ad Testing Strategy

### Create Variations
For each script, create 3 versions:

**Version A:** Focus on "No Monthly Fees"
**Version B:** Focus on "All-in-One Solution"
**Version C:** Focus on specific feature (e.g., Analytics)

### Test Different Hooks (First 3 Seconds)
- "Stop paying monthly fees for invoice software"
- "Tired of spreadsheet chaos?"
- "What if managing your business was this easy?"
- "Join 10,000+ businesses saving time and money"

### Measure Performance
Track:
- View-through rate (VTR)
- Click-through rate (CTR)
- Conversion rate
- Cost per install (CPI)
- Watch time percentage

### Iterate
- Double down on best-performing version
- Test new hooks every 2 weeks
- Refresh creative every 30 days to avoid ad fatigue

## Final Tips

1. **Start Simple:** Begin with screen recordings + voiceover only
2. **Test Before Investing:** Run low-budget tests before professional production
3. **Repurpose Content:** Turn 60s video into 3x 15s clips
4. **Add Captions:** 85% of videos watched without sound
5. **Strong Hook:** First 3 seconds determine if people keep watching
6. **Clear CTA:** Always end with obvious next step
7. **Platform Native:** Videos that look "organic" perform better on social media
