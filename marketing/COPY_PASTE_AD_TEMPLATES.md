## Customization Tips

**Make it yours:**
- Add your specific price point
- Include your actual user count
- Use your real star rating
- Add specific customer testimonials
- Localize for your target market
- Test different value propositions

**Don't:**
- Copy competitors directly
- Make false claims
- Over-promise features
- Use clickbait headlines
- Ignore platform best practices
