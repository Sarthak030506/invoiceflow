# Privacy Policy for InvoiceFlow

**Last updated: [DATE]**

## Introduction

InvoiceFlow ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application InvoiceFlow (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Business Information**: Company name, address, contact details
- **Customer Data**: Customer names, contact information, transaction history
- **Invoice Data**: Invoice details, products/services, pricing, payment information
- **Inventory Data**: Product information, stock levels, pricing

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, app version
- **Usage Data**: App features used, crash reports, performance data
- **Local Storage**: All data is stored locally on your device

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Provide and maintain the App functionality
- Generate invoices and manage business transactions
- Track inventory and customer relationships
- Provide analytics and business insights
- Improve app performance and user experience
- Send notifications about due payments and reminders

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- All your business data is stored locally on your device
- We do not store your business data on our servers
- Data remains under your complete control

### Security Measures
- Data encryption at rest
- Secure local database storage
- Regular security updates
- No unauthorized access to your data

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except:
- **With Your Consent**: When you explicitly choose to share data (e.g., exporting invoices)
- **Legal Requirements**: If required by law or legal process
- **Service Providers**: Minimal data sharing with essential service providers (crash reporting, analytics)

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View all data stored in the app
- **Modify**: Edit or update your information
- **Delete**: Remove data from the app
- **Export**: Export your data in standard formats
- **Backup**: Create local backups of your data

## Children's Privacy

InvoiceFlow is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Third-Party Services

The App may contain links to third-party services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy
